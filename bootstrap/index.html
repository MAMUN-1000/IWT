Divisions of Bangladesh

Dhaka is the capital and one of the major divisions of Bangladesh.

Chattogram is a major coastal division and home to the largest seaport in Bangladesh.

Rajshahi is known for its silk and mangoes, and is an important education hub.

Khulna is close to the Sundarbans, the world's largest mangrove forest.

Barisal is known for its rivers and is often referred to as the Venice of the East.

Sylhet is known for its tea gardens and natural beauty.

Rangpur New City

Rangpur is a northern division, known for agriculture and education.

Mymensingh is a newer division known for agriculture and education institutions.
